ABOUT US


"Welcome to VECTOR GYM, where fitness meets fun! We believe in creating a supportive and motivating environment for individuals of all fitness levels to achieve their health and wellness goals. Our state-of-the-art facility offers a wide range of equipment, personalized training programs, and group fitness classes designed to keep you engaged and motivated on your fitness journey. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just starting out, we're here to help you unlock your full potential and live a healthier, happier life."

PROGRAMMES


  1. Personal Training:

    Our certified personal trainers will work with you one-on-one to create a customized fitness plan tailored to your specific goals and needs. Whether you want to lose weight, build muscle, or improve your overall fitness, our trainers will provide the guidance and support you need to succeed.


  2. Group Fitness Classes:

    From high-intensity interval training (HIIT) to yoga and Pilates, we offer a variety of group fitness classes to keep your workouts fun and exciting. Join our energetic instructors and fellow members for an invigorating workout experience.


  3. Nutrition Counseling:

    Achieving your fitness goals goes hand in hand with proper nutrition. Our nutrition experts will help you develop healthy eating habits, create meal plans, and provide ongoing support to ensure you're fueling your body for success.


  4. Specialized Programs:

    We also offer specialized programs such as boot camps, senior fitness classes, and youth fitness programs to cater to different age groups and fitness levels.


PRICING


  • Membership Plans:

    Choose from flexible membership plans tailored to your needs, including monthly, quarterly, and annual options.


  • Personal Training Packages:

    Invest in your fitness journey with our affordable personal training packages, available in sessions of various durations to fit your schedule and budget.


  • Group Fitness Passes:

    Access our group fitness classes with convenient class pass options or as part of your membership package.


For detailed pricing information and current promotions, please contact our membership team or visit our website.
